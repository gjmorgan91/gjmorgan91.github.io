We considered how this project would integrate to our existing mobile applications. One of those, Insurlink Mobile, is a native app for mobile devices we offer to insurance providers. The application is a portal for their clients to review their policies, connect with their agent, and make changes to their policies. We would integrate with such an application, utilizing machine learning in order to provide a business opportunity for us. This brought us to the focus of mobile development and machine learning as our primary technical goals.
