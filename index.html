About Me
My name is Greg Morgan. I am a soon-to-be graduate at the University of California, San Diego with my BS in Cognitive Science, Human Computer Interaction, with a minor in Computer Science.
I have many passions in life that all stem from my desire to learn to be better designer and visual communicator. From this I have spent my time at UCSD learning about product development, UI design, user experience, information architecture, data visualization and analysis, and branding.
Before my time at UCSD, I studied spatial design, architecture and interior design. Studying, teaching, and practicing I have a combined total of 4 years of experience in human-centered design.
I am the parent to an energetic 2.5 year old golden retriever, named Swarley. I split my time between him, cooking good food, and learning some new skills: currently digital painting.
