I am a designer and developer with over 8 years of experience in the field with a passion for human-centered design.

I live at the intersection of design and technology. As an architect, I've looked towards precedent to learn from the masters while, as a developer, I have looked to the horizon.

I have accomplished projects in several spaces, including professional web applications, graphic design, web development, UX research, and architectural design. Through this work I have become familiar not only with mobile and web platforms but virtual reality as well. Exploring new technologies, sharpening my skills, and building the experiences and opportunities to educate others drives me. The ever changing landscape of design and technology has always pushed me to continue developing and growing to stay up-to-date on trends and tools.

Leadership is a core part of who I am. Diversity in thought, the pursuit of education, and integrity in one's self are what I believe make for strong leadership, and a strong, successful team. I receive an immense amount of fulfillment from leading teams, helping others grow and learn, as well as seeing projects and experiences through from start to finish.
