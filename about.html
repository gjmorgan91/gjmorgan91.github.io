Who I Am

I am a designer and developer with over 7 years of experience in the design and develop fields with a passion for human-centered design.

I have accomplished projects in several spaces, including graphic design, web development, UX research, and architectural design. Through this work I have become familiar not only with mobile and web platforms but virtual reality as well. Exploring new technologies, sharpening my skills, and building the experiences and education of others drives me. The ever changing landscape of design and technology has always pushed me to continue developing and growing to stay up-to-date on trends and tools.

Leadership is a core part of who I am. Diversity in thought, the pursuit of education, and integrity in one's self are what I believe make for strong leadership, and a strong, successful team. I have gotten an immense amount of fulfillment from getting to lead teams, help others grow and learn, and see projects and experiences through from start to finish.

I recently relocated to Denver with my partner and our handsome golden retriever. I am currently looking for new opportunities within Denver's growing tech industry to continue my growth and that of those I get the privledge to work with.
