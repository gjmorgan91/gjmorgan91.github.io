Who I Am

I am a designer and developer with over 7 years of experience. Originally from Los Angeles, California, I am passionate about all things human-centered design.


I have worked on all things ranging from graphic design, web development, ux research, and architectural design getting to work on platforms such as mobile, web, and virtual reality. Learning new technologies, new skills, and interacting with new people keep me going. With the ever-changing landscape of design and technologies, I am always looking to learn, stay up to date, and push myself.


Leadership is a core part of who I am and what pushes me. I have gotten an immense amount of fulfillment from getting to lead teams, help others grow and learn, and see projects and experiences through from start to finish.


Recently, I relocated to the Greater Denver Area with my partner and our handsome golden retriever. I am currently looking for new opportunities within Denver's growing tech scene within project management, bringing my passion and skills to others.
